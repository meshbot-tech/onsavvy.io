How it works

From link to confirmed booking in seconds

A smooth, low-friction booking experience for your clients — zero phone tag, zero spreadsheets.

1
Client visits your booking page

A branded, mobile-optimised booking page — embedded on your site or shared as a direct link via WhatsApp, email, or Instagram bio.

2
Picks a service, date & time

They select the service they need, browse your real-time availability, and pick a slot — no back-and-forth, no phone calls required.

3
Instant confirmation sent

Automated confirmation via email and/or WhatsApp the moment they book. Calendar invite attached. Reminder sent 24 hours before.

4
You manage from your dashboard

View all upcoming bookings, reschedule, cancel, or add notes — all from a clean admin dashboard synced to your Google Calendar.

Built-in capabilities

Everything scheduling needs

Purpose-built for service businesses — not a calendar plugin bolted onto something else.

Real-Time Availability

Calendar blocks update live — no double bookings, no manual management. Syncs with Google Calendar and Outlook.

Multi-Staff Scheduling

Assign services to specific team members. Each staff member has their own availability, buffer times, and booking limits.

Automated Reminders

Confirmation, 24-hour reminder, and follow-up messages sent automatically via email and WhatsApp. Reduce no-shows by up to 60%.

Intake Forms

Collect client information before the appointment — custom questions, file uploads, consent forms — filled in at booking, not on the day.

Buffer & Prep Times

Set preparation and cleanup time between appointments automatically — so your team is never double-booked or rushed between clients.

Service Menu

Configurable service list with name, duration, price, description, and assigned staff — clients see exactly what they're booking and at what cost.

Deposit & Payment

Require a deposit at booking via M-Pesa or card — so clients are committed and you're protected against last-minute cancellations.

Embeddable Anywhere

Drop a booking widget onto any page of your website, or share a direct booking link. Works on all devices, no app download required.
